A 7-day take-home ↑ Day 3 hit on Day 1 · 3× plan pace

A meeting recorder that runs entirely on the iPhone.

You hit record. The phone listens, writes the transcript as people talk, and afterward hands you a clean summary of what was decided, who agreed to do what, and what was left open. Then you ask it questions out loud and it answers, also out loud — without ever touching the internet. The plan budgeted three days for this milestone. It shipped in one.
